Team 7A consisting of Carlsen, Arshad, Kai Mun, Jourdan, Nasruddine and Matthew from Singapore. Our project is SmartSave.

It eliminates financial confusion, and optimal savings can be achieved seamlessly. A new way to maximize savings is now possible with SmartSave, where renowned banks' best interest rates are extensively explored, funds are seamlessly transferred between accounts, and opening a new account takes seconds. Through SmartSave, the user's savings journey is elegantly streamlined, allowing them to see their assets flourish like never before.
